How This Works

01.

You complete and submit the inquiry form.

02.

A representative of Dragonfly contacts you within three business days by email or phone.

03.

A 30–60 minute discovery call is scheduled to confirm goals, scope, timeline, and budget.

04.

You send requested documents; we confirm details.

05.

We deliver a written proposal with scope, milestones, costs, and terms.

06.

You review and finalize the proposal; you sign the contract and pay the deposit if required.

07.

You attend the kickoff meeting and complete onboarding.

08.

We execute the project and send regular status updates and milestone reports.

09.

You review final deliverables and approve for final payment.

10.
