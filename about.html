Baltai Daniel

Founder & CEO

Baltai Daniel is Dragonfly's Founder and CEO. He leads the company's engineering division, where teams develop innovative software and interactive experiences. He exemplifies a new generation of tech leadership, bringing exceptional maturity and vision to oversee the company's strategic direction and day-to-day operations.

Baltai Daniel leads Dragonfly's engineering division, where his teams develop innovative software and interactive experiences that define the company's offerings. With over 4 years of hands-on experience in software and video game development, he has built a diverse technical skillset across multiple programming languages—including C++, Python, JavaScript, and GDScript (Godot). Entirely self-taught since he began coding at age 12, his ability to master these languages independently demonstrates both technical depth and self-directed learning capability. He specializes in 3D modeling and animation, skills he leverages strategically to advance Dragonfly's entertainment sector.

Before founding Dragonfly, Baltai Daniel gained experience through multiple ventures. He worked as a consultant for an organization, developing an educational video game for out-of-school children—a project that underscored his commitment to using technology for social impact. He also served as software developer for a startup non-profit that shut down in 2023 due to team mismanagement, an experience that shaped his understanding of organizational dynamics and leadership. Additionally, he has worked as a freelancer, building further experience in client-driven development.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Baltai Daniel maintains a well-rounded lifestyle, exploring music and art creatively while remaining fiercely competitive on the football field—channeling the same drive and intensity he brings to engineering.
