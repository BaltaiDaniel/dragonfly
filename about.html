Who We Are

What We Do

We deliver innovative experiences through software and entertainment (video games and animations) that transform the way technology is accessed and experienced.

Our Mission

Our Vision

How We Work

This section outlines how we treat each other at work.

We operate as a fully distributed team across multiple locations, proving that geography is no barrier to exceptional work. Our asynchronous-first approach ensures projects move forward without delays.

We build trust across differences. Age, background, geography, accent—none of it changes how we respect and value each other. Diverse perspectives make our work stronger and our team better.

Every project, deadline, and deliverable has clear ownership and measurable outcomes. We document decisions and progress transparently so there's never ambiguity about where things stand or who to reach out to.

Remote doesn't mean distant. We're a team that genuinely celebrates wins, supports each other through challenges, and brings our whole selves to our work. This translates directly into better collaboration with our team—we listen and we care about each other's success.

Transparency isn't about perfection—it's about honesty. We communicate setbacks as openly as wins. And we genuinely support each other through the hard stuff.

Our greatest advantage is that we're a young team; we have enough time to fail, learn, and pick ourselves back up again.

’’

- Baltai Daniel, Founder & CEO

Baltai Daniel

Founder & CEO

Baltai Daniel is Dragonfly's Founder and CEO. He leads Dragonfly's engineering division, where his teams develop innovative software and interactive experiences that define the company's offerings. With over 4 years of hands-on experience in software and video game development, he has built a diverse technical skillset across multiple programming languages—including C/C++, Python, and JavaScript. Entirely self-taught since he began coding at age 12, his ability to master these languages independently demonstrates both technical depth and self-directed learning capability. He specializes in 3D modeling and animation, skills he leverages strategically to advance Dragonfly's entertainment sector.

Before founding Dragonfly, Baltai Daniel gained experience through multiple ventures. He worked as a consultant for an organization, developing an educational video game for out-of-school children—a project that underscored his commitment to using technology for social impact. He also served as software developer for a startup non-profit that shut down in 2023 due to team mismanagement, an experience that shaped his understanding of organizational dynamics and leadership. Additionally, he has worked as a freelancer, building further experience in client-driven development.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Baltai Daniel maintains a well-rounded lifestyle, exploring music and art creatively while remaining fiercely competitive on the football field—channeling the same drive and intensity he brings to engineering.
