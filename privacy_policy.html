Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7th March, 2026

This Privacy Policy delineates the personal data that Dragonfly collects, how we process it, and our purposes for doing so. This Policy encompasses all Dragonfly products, both physical and virtual such as our services, websites, and software. Dragonfly may provide links to third-party services. Kindly note that Dragonfly’s Privacy Policy does not extend to the practices of these third parties regarding personal data. We strongly advise you to review their privacy policies and understand your privacy rights before engaging with them. We urge you to carefully read the product-specific sections of this Privacy Policy, which contain vital information pertinent to each product. This Policy governs all interactions you have with Dragonfly and the Dragonfly products listed herein, as well as any other Dragonfly products that present this Policy.


Collection of Personal Data

Types of Data: Dragonfly collects various types of personal data, including but not limited to:

- Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number.

- Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address.

- Device Identifiers: IP addresses, unique device identifiers.

- Location Data: Geographic location data.


Sources of Data: Data is collected directly from users when they:

- Create an account on our platform.

- Purchase products or services from us.

- Download and use our applications.

- Interact with our customer support.

We may also collect data from third-party sources and through automated means such as cookies.


Use of Personal Data

Service Provision: Dragonfly uses the collected data to:

- Provide and improve our products and services.

- Process transactions and manage customer accounts.

- Offer customer support and respond to inquiries.


Personalization: We use personal data to:

- Personalize content and user experience.

- Provide recommendations and targeted advertising.


Security: Personal data helps us:

- Enhance the security of our products and services.

- Detect and prevent fraud and other malicious activities.


Sharing of Personal Data

Third Parties: Dragonfly may share personal data with:

- Service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processors and data storage services.

- Business partners for joint marketing and product development efforts.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose personal data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.


Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal data. Your personal data is stored in secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.


Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.


Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- The right to access the personal data we hold about you.

- The right to request the correction of inaccurate personal data.

- The right to request the deletion of your personal data.

- The right to object to the processing of your personal data.

- The right to request the restriction of processing of your personal data.

- The right to data portability.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

Dragonfly reserves the right to update or change our privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on our website. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
